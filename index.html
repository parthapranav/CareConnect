Platform Features
Everything you need,
all in one place.
From smart volunteer matching to real-time communication and impact dashboards — CareConnect covers it all.

Smart Matching System

Our AI-powered algorithm matches volunteers with orphanages based on skills, location, and availability — ensuring the right people connect.

💬

Real-time Communication

In-platform messaging, notifications, and activity updates keep all stakeholders aligned throughout the volunteering journey.

📊

Impact Dashboards

Track volunteer hours, children helped, activities completed, and donations — all in transparent, shareable reports.

Verified & Safe
